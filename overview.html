A little explanation

About

This website was designed and developed by Abisai "Sai" Sem,
for the sole aim of showcasing artists' work on a digital platform, other than social networking applications.
The artist featured in this version of the site has agreed to having his likeness and precious work used.

Expansion

Plans to expand the site include having different forms of visual art showcased on this site.
Visual art including but not limited to photography, short films and animations.
Different artist will have the oppurtunity to contribute their work to the site and aid in its' growth.

The expansion of this site to accommodate more than one artist and art type, will see a small restructuring
of a few of the pages. This restructuring will make it so each artist will have a personal profile with a breif biography.
Art on the site will then be split into categories for easy navigation.

Intellectual Property

The art work and website itself are protected under intellectual property rights,
and any attempt to mimic, duplicate or other wise plagiarize the work on this site will be considered kind of messed up,
so please don't do that. Refrain from taking screenshots and using them any where else without explicit permission.

Enjoy the deep dive into an artistic soul : )
