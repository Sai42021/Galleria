The Artist

Hiholiwe MM Immanuel

Hiholiwe MM Immanuel is a Namibian national born in January 2001 and
currently resides in Oshakati. He ventured into digital arts at the age of 17
as his first form of art, never drew on visual canvas before. As he began with
his art roller-coaster he is not yet at a career level but looking forward to it.
Indulge in the gallery of Hiholiwe and the only place his soul lays.
